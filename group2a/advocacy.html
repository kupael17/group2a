HOME

We are advocating the Barangay New Lucban Good Governance for people to increase awareness in doing the right act in governing. It is an important aspect since it a key to success.Good governance is how the public institutions manage resources and give actions for the development of the Community.

The collaboration between of this public institution is important because it gives people satisfaction with the services they need. A country needs a good leader, a leader who could deliver and facilitate the necessities of the community in a way that all the resources are managed and utilized well. Delivering the right service can build trust to the people. By supporting the programs of the government, we can make our life better. Good decisions can change one individual life. When choosing a leader in your community, consider the attitude, because it reflects on how they will work.

God Governance will be effective by :

CHOOSING RIGHT LEADERS

Each of us has the right to choose who will be elected as the official in Our community. In the election, we should select the right person to take charge of that Position. Take note that selecting the right people is the key to successful leadership.

UNITY BETWEEN INSTITUTION

By supporting all the programs implemented, the community will lead to the path of peace and harmony.

COOPERATION OF THE COMMUNITY

People should support and do all the rules and regulation, so that the The community will free from problems. Officials are the one who is in charge of making laws and implementing it for the people to follow it for their own goods.

COMMITMENT TO WORK

A leader who does not have passion to their work will result to poor attention on the needs of people. You as a leader should provide a goal to have a target and direction for your country to progress.
